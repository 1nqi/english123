Pacific "Ring of Fire"

This region covers about 75% of all earthquakes in the world. It includes countries along the Pacific coast such as Japan, Indonesia, Chile and Alaska.
Ring of fire wiki article

Tsunamis often occur in subduction zones where one tectonic plate sinks under another. Examples include the Pacific "Ring of Fire" and other regions where subduction occurs. Earthquakes in the sea or ocean can cause a tsunami. This can happen in different parts of the world where earthquakes occur.

Japan

A country in east asia

On average, more than 1,500 earthquakes of magnitude 4 or higher occur annually in Japan, which cause a tsunami.

Vanuatu

a country in the South Pacific

Every year in Vanuatu there are several hundred earthquakes of magnitude 7 and higher, which cause a tsunami.

Philippines

a country in southeast asia

Every year in the Philippines there are several dozen tsunamis that kill several hundred people
